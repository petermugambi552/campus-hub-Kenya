    FAQ

    Frequently asked questions about CampusHub Kenya features and delivery.

    • How long is delivery?

      Delivery is designed to arrive within 3 days for campus zones.

    • Can students resell products?

      Yes—students can post used items, set prices and earn commission from the platform.

    • Is M-Pesa supported?

      Yes. The checkout experience simulates M-Pesa payment initiation for Kenyan users.
